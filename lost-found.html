How It Works

Our community-driven platform makes it easy to reunite lost items with their owners

01

Report Your Lost Item

Click "Report Lost Item" and fill in the details. Include a clear description, last known location, and any unique features that could help identify your item.

Tip: Adding a photo increases your chances by 70%
Quick Form
02

Your Item Gets Listed

Once submitted, your lost item appears in our searchable database. Community members in your area are notified to keep an eye out.

We'll notify you of any potential matches
Live Listing
03

Connect with Finders

When someone finds your item, they can contact you directly through our secure messaging system. Verify the item details before arranging pickup.

All communications are secure and private
Safe Connect
04

Reunited & Happy

Meet in a safe, public location to retrieve your item. Once reunited, mark your item as found to help us track success stories!

We suggest meeting at local police stations or malls
